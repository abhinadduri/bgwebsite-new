Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Senior Portrait? How do I take one?

Graduating seniors at Cal take their senior portraits for inclusion in the B&G Yearbook. Only seniors who take their portraits will be included in the yearbook. Sitting fees start at $5. Packages for purchasing portraits are sold by our photography company, Lauren Studios.

If you are graduating in December 2019, May 2020, or Summer 2020, sign-up to take your senior portraits today! All students who have senior standing should have received an email from Lauren Studios that includes a personal client-ID. Use this client-ID to make an appointment online!

I took my senior portraits, now what?

You will receive an email from Lauren Studios in 2-3 weeks once your proofs are available to view online. There, you can select your “yearbook photo”. Physical proofs will also be mailed to you in 3-4 weeks. If you are interested in purchasing photo packages, you may do so through Lauren Studios.

Meanwhile, you can purchase the 2019 B&G Yearbook!

What are the senior portrait sitting options?

5 poses | $5 | 5 poses for yearbook photo
10 poses | $15 | 5 poses for yearbook photo, 5 additional poses: cap + gown or casual outfits
15 poses | $25 | 5 poses for yearbook photo, 5 cap + gown, 5 casual outfits

How much does the 2018-2019 yearbook cost?

Normally, the yearbook is priced at 100 dollars. If you purchase a yearbook along with your senior portraits, they are 70 dollars.

How can I purchase older yearbooks?

Please contact us with your order request and we will check our archives to see if we have copies of the yearbook you’ve selected.

When will I receive my yearbook?

The Blue & Gold Yearbook is a Fall release book, which means it is printed and published over the summer for release sometime in the Fall Semester.

If you opted to pick up your yearbook, you will receive an email from when it is ready for pick up on campus.
If you opted to have the book shipped to you, you will receive an email from us once your book has been shipped.

I ordered a yearbook and it was never sent to me!

Our default option is for books to be picked up on campus; unless you paid an additional shipping and handling charge, then you should receive an e-mail when the book is available for pickup. If you would like to inquire about getting your yearbook delivered to you, please contact us.

I am not satisfied with my senior portrait. Can I retake it?

Yes, of course! Retakes are free for all Seniors.

About B&G


The Yearbook

The Blue & Gold Yearbook is the official yearbook for the University of California, Berkeley. Published since 1875, the Blue & Gold provides readers coverage of events, issues and trends within the Berkeley community. As a historical record, the yearbook also includes campus facts, figures and people, including a section for graduating seniors’ portraits and majors.


Our Mission

Our mission is to truthfully encapsulate the current, unique Berkeley identity — its culture, events, and lifestyle — as students, for the students.


The Organization

The Blue & Gold Yearbook staff is comprised of over fifty individuals who design, produce, market, and distribute the book. Financially self-sufficient, the organization earns its revenue without aid of grants or outside funding. The Associated Students of the University of California (ASUC student government) formally sponsors the organization and provides it with office space.
