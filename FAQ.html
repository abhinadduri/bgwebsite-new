Frequently Asked Questions


Who appears in the Yearbook?

All students who get their Senior Portraits (see below) taken during the 2019-2020 Academic Year will appear in the 2019-2020 edition of the Yearbook.

Additionally, seniors can apply to have their college stories told for the Yearbook's Senior Features section.

I haven’t gotten my yearbook, when will I receive it?

Our books cover Spring Commencement, so they come out in the subsequent Fall semester. The 2019-2020 Yearbook will be available sometime during the Fall 2020 semester .

If you selected on campus pick-up, you will receive an email when they are ready.

If you are not waiting for the book to be released or otherwise have concerns regarding the whereabouts of your book, feel free to contact info.bgyearbook@gmail.com!

How can I purchase the Yearbook?

You can purchase all editions of the Yearbook from our Online Store .

You can purchase the 2019-2020 Yearbook at a 50% Discount with the receptionist at your Senior Portrait session .

What is a Senior Portrait?

Graduating seniors can take their senior portraits to be put into the Yearbook.

Sitting fees for these portrait sessions start at $5.

The portrait packages themselves are available for purchase through our photo studio company, Lauren Studios.

How do I sign up for Senior Portraits?

If you are a graduating senior, Lauren Studios should send you an email at some point throughout the academic year with instructions on how to sign up for senior portraits.

Sitting fees for these portrait sessions start at $5.

If you didn’t receive this email or would like to sign up immediately, please email info.bgyearbook@gmail.com for instructions.

When and where can I take Portraits?

Lauren Studios takes the portraits out of our office in the MLK Student Union Mondays through Fridays.

Portrait sessions should be available most of the semester, with the last day of photos being the Friday before dead week.

To get to the office, follow the signs in MLK with directions to “Blue & Gold Yearbook”. It's on the bottom floor, down the long hallway from the Student Store.

I took my Senior Portraits, now what?

In 2-3 weeks, Lauren Studios should send you an email containing image files of the portraits you took. You’ll be asked to select one to be featured in the Yearbook.

If you don’t like the pictures taken of you, you can sign up for retakes for free. Simply sign up for another session the way you did previously.

How can I get involved in Blue & Gold Yearbook?

We recruit every semester for new talents for our journalism, photography, design, and business teams. Click here for more details.
